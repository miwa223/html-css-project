Maikohan

An apprentice geisha in Kyoto.

Maiko are the girls who are training to become Geiko. Their day-to-day training is extremely tough, but they make every effort to reach their dream of becoming independent professionals.

How to makeup

Base

Using a flat type makeup brush called Hake, you cover up the whole face and neck. with the mixture of water and Oshiroi, white makeup powder.

Eyeblow

Blur two colors of red and black with a special brush.

Eyehole

Apply red eyeshadows and a black eyeliner on eyeholes.

Lip

Only the bottom lip of a maiko is painted red (A senior maiko will have half of their upper lip painted red)
